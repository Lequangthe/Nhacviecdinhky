MainTask helps you never forget your recurring tasks: oil changes, vehicle inspection, insurance renewal, filter changes, backups…

Set each task with its interval (every X days, weekly or monthly) and MainTask reminds you before it's due.

Features:

* Recurring tasks: fixed intervals, weekly or monthly
* One-shot tasks, with or without a target date
* Completion history for each task
* Customizable notifications (3 days or 1 day before, at the time you choose)
* "Done" and "Snooze" buttons right inside notifications
* Scrollable home screen widget
* Quick Settings tile
* 5 color themes + dark mode
* Backup export / import (history included)
* Available in English and French

Privacy-friendly: no ads, no trackers, no account. Everything stays on your device.
